About Me

Who am I?

Hi there! I'm Seamus, a super-friendly Yorkshire Terrier and Bichon Frise mix, and let me tell you, I’m one happy doggo who loves everyone and everything! My hooman parents are Cillian (he’s from Dublin) and Kirsty (she’s from Liverpool). They spoil me rotten with tons of cuddles and attention—even when I’m being a bit barky and loud (hey, I’m just expressing myself!). My absolute favorite place in the world is Nefyn in North Wales!

My hoomans have been going there for years, but I think I love it the most. There are so many beaches where I can run free, chase birds, and play with other dogs. Sometimes, my cousins Magic and Merlin are there too with their hoomans. Merlin and I are the “bad boys”—we get up to all kinds of mischief, while Magic just sits quietly with Molly and Hattie. Boring, right? 😂 When I'm not at the beach, you’ll find me at Penny Lane Dog Park, hanging out with my besties. Freddie (a wire-haired collie) and Tuco (I’m not sure what breed he is, but he’s a blast) are my regular buds. Alfie the whippet is fun too, but he’s so fast I can never quite catch him. 😢 My favorite, though, is Hope, a golden retriever. She’s not always there, but when she is, I know it’s going to be a good play day! My hoomans are thrilled too—they know I’ll be all tuckered out after. 😆
